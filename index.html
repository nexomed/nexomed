What We Do

Technology for Better Care

Self-Service Health Kiosk

A kiosk that guides patients through key health measurement steps independently, without needing clinical assistance at each stage.

Accessible & Inclusive Design

Designed for simplicity and usability so that patients across a broad range of ages and abilities can use it.

Reduced Waiting Times

By automating routine measurements, the technology is intended to help reduce time spent on administrative tasks.

Community & Clinic Settings

Designed for use in a range of community healthcare environments, including GP surgeries, pharmacies, and outpatient departments.
